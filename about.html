About

Josh Molnar

Founder of Bitcoin Daily · also known as Bitcoin Jae

Josh Molnar is a full-time trader and the founder of Bitcoin Daily, one of the largest Bitcoin-focused communities online, reaching more than 500,000 people across social media. He trades crypto and futures for a living, including funded prop firm accounts, and teaches others to do the same.

His path into crypto was anything but conventional, it started in music. Josh left high school early to fast-track a music career, earned his GED, and went on to study audio engineering. The bet paid off: he was signed as both a recording artist and a producer, spending years writing, producing, and engineering records.

Along the way he worked whatever paid the bills between sessions, driving for Uber, delivering food, until 2017, when he found the thing that would consume him the way music once had: Bitcoin. He brought the same self-taught obsession to the markets, dedicating himself to learning the technology, the charts, and the discipline of trading. That obsession became Bitcoin Daily.

Building Bitcoin Daily

Bitcoin Daily began as a simple Instagram page where Josh shared market analysis and his day-to-day thoughts on crypto. It resonated. The page grew into one of the biggest Bitcoin accounts on Instagram, and from there into a full brand: a YouTube channel, a community, a merch store, and a premium analysis group.

Today the mission is the same as day one: make crypto understandable for normal people. Josh focuses on education over hype: teaching people how to read the market, manage risk, and think for themselves, in an industry full of noise and bad actors.

Trading for a living

Behind the brand, Josh is an active trader first. He trades crypto and futures intraday, and trades funded prop firm accounts, capital provided by trading firms to traders who can prove a disciplined, risk-managed edge. It is the unglamorous, professional side of trading: a documented process, fixed risk on every position, and treating a string of losses as the cost of doing business rather than a reason to blow up an account.

That is also what he teaches. Through one-on-one and group mentorship, Josh works with people who want to take trading seriously as a craft, building a repeatable process, passing prop firm challenges the right way, and learning to think in probabilities instead of chasing the next hot call.

What Josh focuses on

  • Day trading crypto and futures with a documented, risk-first process
  • Trading funded prop firm accounts, and teaching others to pass and manage them
  • One-on-one and group mentorship for serious, aspiring traders
  • Honest, data-driven market analysis through Bitcoin Daily

Josh Molnar (also known as Bitcoin Jae) is a cryptocurrency educator, trader, and the founder of Bitcoin Daily, one of the largest Bitcoin communities online. A former signed recording artist and producer who studied audio engineering, Josh brought the same self-taught discipline to the markets and launched Bitcoin Daily in 2017. Today the brand reaches more than 500,000 people across Instagram, YouTube, and its trading community, with a focus on education over hype.

Josh Molnar (also known as Bitcoin Jae) is a data scientist, trader, and the founder of Bitcoin Daily, one of the largest Bitcoin communities online, and the author of "Bitcoin Runs on a Clock," a study of Bitcoin's four-year halving cycle available on SSRN. A former signed recording artist and producer who studied audio engineering, he taught himself to trade years before founding Bitcoin Daily in 2017. Today the brand reaches more than 500,000 people across Instagram, YouTube, and its trading community, with a focus on data and education over hype.

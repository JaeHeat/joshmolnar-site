The Brand

What is Bitcoin Daily?

A crypto education brand founded by Josh Molnar in 2017, built to make Bitcoin understandable for everyday people.

Bitcoin Daily is a cryptocurrency education brand founded by Josh Molnar in 2017. What began as a single Instagram page sharing daily market thoughts has grown into a community of more than 500,000 people across Instagram, YouTube, and beyond.

The idea is simple: crypto is intimidating, full of jargon, hype, and outright scams. Bitcoin Daily cuts through that with clear, consistent, education-first content, so you can actually understand what's happening in the market and make your own informed decisions.

What you get from Bitcoin Daily

  • Daily market analysis: Bitcoin and the broader crypto market, explained without the hype.
  • Trading education: how to read charts, manage risk, and avoid the mistakes that wipe out most new traders.
  • Premium analysis group: deeper chart breakdowns and detailed trade ideas with clear entries, targets, and stop-losses.
  • A community: half a million people learning the space together.

The Bitcoin Daily ecosystem
